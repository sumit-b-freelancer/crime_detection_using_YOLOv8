System Workflow

How CRIMSON processes and responds to potential threats

1

Video Feed Acquisition

System connects to surveillance cameras (CCTV, body cams, drones) and begins real-time video stream processing. Supports RTSP, HTTP streams, and local video files.

2

Frame Analysis & Detection

Each video frame is processed through multiple AI models simultaneously: facial recognition for criminals, YOLO for weapons, and pose estimation for violent behavior.

3

Threat Verification

Potential matches are cross-verified against criminal databases and analyzed for confidence levels. Multiple frames are examined to reduce false positives.

4

Alert Generation

Verified threats trigger automated alerts containing suspect images, last known location, weapon details, and suggested response protocols.

5

Evidence Archiving

All detection events are automatically logged with timestamps, GPS coordinates, and video evidence stored in encrypted databases for investigative use.
